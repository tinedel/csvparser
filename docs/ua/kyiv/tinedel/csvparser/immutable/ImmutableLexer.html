    Immutable CSV parser and which relies on immutable tokenizer and lexer to perform the job

    Immutable CSV parser and which relies on immutable tokenizer and lexer to perform the job

    ImmutableTokenizer - Takes input scala.io.Source and splits it into tokens ImmutableLexer - Processes CSV grammar to get stream of lexems according to csv standard ImmutableParser - Finalizes the processing producing Map of field number (1-based) to field content

    Instances of Lexer and Parser are capable of taking non-string Streams to implement parsers with smaller memory consumption for the case of huge values in the cells.
